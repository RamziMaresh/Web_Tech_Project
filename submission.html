All paper submissions will be handled electronically. Papers must be submitted electronically in DOC format at easychair.
Submitted papers must not substantially overlap with papers that have been published or that are simultaneously submitted
to a journal or a conference with proceedings. Submitted papers will be peer-reviewed and carefully evaluated based
on originality, significance, technical soundness, and clarity of exposition. The submitted papers must be between
4-6 pages. To facilitate the paper’s double-blind peer review process, authors should make efforts to ensure that
information about the authors’ identities do not appear anywhere in the text-body of the paper and must follow the guidelines.
Upon acceptance, at least one of the authors listed in the paper must submit the final paper, attend the
conference and present the paper according to the schedule of the conference. In the event that the presenter is unable to
attend the conference for any reasons, your accepted paper will not be considered for submission of the proceeding to the
IEEE Xplore. Accepted papers after further revisions will be invited for publications in selected journals. Please direct
any questions about the paper submission process to the program chair at ict4m@iium.edu.my.
Papers that are accepted must be presented at the conference, either by the authors themselves or via a proxy. In case a paper
is not presented at the conference, it shall be deemed a ‘no-show’. Only presented papers will be submitted for indexing. No refund
of the paid fees may be claimed by the ‘no-show’ author.
We would like to invite you to submit your papers to our International Conference on Information and Communication Technology
for the Muslim World 2018 (ICT4M 2018) to be held in International Islamic University Malaysia (IIUM).
