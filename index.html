ICT4M

ICT4M 2020 is a conference that gathers academicians, researchers, practitioners, professionals, and students to address the challenges of Information and Communication Technology (ICT) for the Muslim world. This conference has been conducted seven times so far with the most recent location in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia on 23-25 March 2020. In conjunction with the World Congress on Integration and Islamicization, ICT4M 2020 will be held on 27-29 March 2020 in Kuantan, Pahang.
